About

DAL UNICEF is a university club at Dalhousie University that advocates for UNICEF's mission of supporting children's rights worldwide.

The club raises awareness and organizes events to promote quality education, healthcare, and protection for children, aligning with UNICEF's goals. Through fundraisers, campaigns, and partnerships, DAL UNICEF empowers students to make a positive impact and contribute to a better future for children globally.
